SNUIPS Workshop

  • 2026.02.03
Description :

Seoul National University Institute of Psychological Science(SNUIPS) opened a winter workshop, "Build Your Own AI Co-Scientist" for psychology graduate students on February 3rd.

Professor Jiook Cha opened the workshop, emphashizing the importance of learning AI in this rapidly developing society.
Yoonkyung Lee introduced the Workshop, introducing AI agents.
Seonu An, our MA student, ran the first hands-on session, "An Introduction to RAG Through Chatbot Experiment Design with Streamlit". She introduced the basics of Retrieval-Augmented Generation and ran a demo using Streamlit.
Serin Oh, our MA student, and Yujin Yun introduced how to use AI in data processing. They ran an "Automated EDA Pipelines" hands-on session, suggesting a way to use AI in data analysis.
Yejin Lee ran "AI agents for Manuscript Writing and Peer Review", introducing how to use AI effectively from literature review to writing a draft.

Special thanks to Joohye Lee, Heekang Lim, and Jaehyeong Lee, who assisted with the workshop.
