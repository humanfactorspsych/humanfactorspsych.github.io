Project detail

We are excited to officially launch the Active Aging HAI Center (AAH Center)!
  • 2025.06
Description :

The AAH Center has been established as part of Seoul National University's 2025 AI Research Support Program, with funding from the Seoul National University AI Institute.

Under the leadership of Prof. Sowon Hahn (Head, aging and human factors), our interdisciplinary team includes professors from SNU: Hyunji Kwon (sociology), Kawon Kim (industrial/organizational psychology), Yeonsoo Kim (sports medicine), Juyeon Park (human factors and wearable technology), Bongwon Suh (human-centered computing), Jooeun Ahn (sports engineering), Joonhwan Lee (human computer interaction), Haeyeon Lee (developmental psychology), and Jung-Hwa Ha (social welfare), along with Dr. Yoon Kyung Lee (cognitive psychology) and our dedicated graduate student researchers.

The center will spearhead cutting-edge research initiatives focused on active aging and human-AI interaction.
We look forward to advancing this important field through innovative, collaborative research!
