Description :

Yoon Kyung is a first-year Ph.D. student in Cognitive Psychology at Seoul National University. She received her Sc.B. in Psychology from the University of Iowa in 2015 and M.A. in Cognitive Psychology from Seoul National University. She is interested in computational modeling of social and emotional intelligence for an autonomous and intelligent artificial agents.She uses machine learning methods to model human behavior in human-robot interactions as well. She is also interested in simulating human-agent interaction in both virtual reality. In her free time, she enjoyings hiking, cooking, and blogging.
