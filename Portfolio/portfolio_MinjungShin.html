Minjung Shin

Description :

Minjung Shin is a Ph.D. Candidate in Interdisciplinary Program in Cognitive Science at Seoul National University. She received her B.S. in Biological Science from the Ewha Womans University in 2000 and M.S. in Neurobiology from the Ewha Womans University in 2002. She had taken a long hiatus after finishing her coursework for the Ph.D. degree from Interdisciplinary Program in Cognitive Science at SNU in 2005. Now she is interested in the intelligence of humans and machines based on human cognition with working as a Research Assistant at Dongguk University in parallel with her Ph.D. Outside of the lab, she is a sweet mom of two kids and enjoys reading and writing.

Current Projects:

- Enhancing the Ethics of Data Characteristics and Generation AI Models for Social and Ethical Learning (by IITP). 2024-Present. - Development of Object-Oriented Synthetic Data Generation and Evaluation Methods (by IITP). 2023-Present. - Development of Uncertainty-Aware Agents Learning by Asking Questions (by IITP). 2022-Present. - Smart Aging Avatars: Research on Intelligent Biofeedback and Visualized Human-AI Interaction Technologies (by SNU Interdisciplinary Research Project). 2022. - Beyond the Turing Test, Multi-modal Story Memory Structure,Video Intelligence Platform (VIP),Video QA & Problem Solving ,Video Story Learning and Inference (by IITP). 2021.
- Brain Inspired AI Framework and Cognitive Convergence RnD pipeline based Health Behavior Monitoring, Diagnosis and Prescription Technology (by IITP). 2020-2021.
- Neural mechanisms and interaction of motor efference copy and sensory feedback (by NRF). 2020-2021.
