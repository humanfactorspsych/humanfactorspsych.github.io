RA (intern) Recruitment
  • - 2024.12. 27.
Description :

We are currently recruiting undergraduate research assistants/interns for 2024-2025 winter/spring. We conduct many interdisciplinary research projects on Social and Empathic AI, Human-AI/Robot Interaction, AI for Healthcare, UI/UX.

  1. Qualifications
    1. Proficiency with programming languages (Python, R) for AI, Large scale data, and Statistical analysis
    2. Willing to work 12 hours a week, for one semester at the least
    3. Responsible, cooperative and strict about deadlines
    4. Both recent undergraduate students / alumni are applicable
    5. Available to work offline in lab (1 - 2 days per week)

  2. Responsibility
    1. Participation in lab meetings (offline, biweekly)
    2. Participation in weekly journal clubs/methodology workshop (offline, biweekly)
    3. Assistance in lab research projects
      • 1. literature review
      • 2. data collecting, preprocessing, and coding
      • 3. data labelling for AI
      • 4. participating in usability tests

  3. Preferred Qualifications
    1. Having taken psychological statistics and research methodology classes
    2. Proficiency with big data/deep learning/machine learning/large language model (Advantage for practical experience)
    3. Have experience with tools that are used for UI/UX and Virtual Reality (VR) (Including Unity, Unreal Engine, Blender-3D, Figma etc.)

  4. How to Apply
    1. Fill out the application form.
    2. Send an email to Professor Hahn(swhahn@snu.ac.kr) and to the lab manager (celineoh@snu.ac.kr), with the following attachments:
      1. Copy of the RA application form
      2. CV(pdf or url)
      3. programming portfolio (free format including github repository, ipynb notebook, coursework certificate)

      * The title of your email should be [RA application]

  5. Number of opening position: 0
  6. Application Deadline: December 20, 2024
  7. Recruitment Process
    1. Document Screening
    2. Interview(Only qualified applicants will be contacted for an interview)

      3. *Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis


*Do not send the transcript.
*Your experience as a research assistant in our lab does not guarantee acceptance to graduate school.
