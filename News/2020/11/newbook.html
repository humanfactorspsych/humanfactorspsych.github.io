New Book Release: Brain Plasticity that Changes a Life
  • 2020.11
Description :

The brain constantly changes. When I change, the brain changes, which can lead a life to change. This book is not only about research data but also about the author's life stories. While other scientific books provide theoretical explanations on the power of the brain, this story contains the author's personal experiences from fighting cancer to prosopagnosia to explain brain plasticity.
