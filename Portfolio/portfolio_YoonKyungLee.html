Research Interests

Yoon Kyung’s research focus is on the development and application of empathetic AI, specifically in the domains of social robots, healthcare, and AI. She uses computational methods (deep learning, NLP, and machine learning) and quantitative methods. Her work has been published in several venues, including CogSci, IEEE IROS, and AAAI HCOMP.

Topics: Empathy, Human-AI Interaction, Healthcare, Social and Emotional Intelligence, Computational Modeling, AI, Machine Theory-of-Mind

Community Engagement:

Yoon Kyung has organized local meetups for CogSci in 2022 in Seoul [link]. She has also organized various workshops and a journal club within HFPsych Lab (e.g., Journal Club, Programming Workshops, and “Applied Cognition(AppCog)” Seminars).
